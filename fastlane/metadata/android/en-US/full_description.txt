Ark is an offline-first survival computer for Android.

It helps you prepare useful phone data before connectivity fails: offline maps, routing packs, first-aid and survival references, ZIM knowledge archives, private notes, imported documents, cached weather and RSS, local source search, and practical field tools.

Core data stays on device unless you export or share it. Ark has no backend account system, no ads, no analytics, and no cloud sync.

Current beta limitations:

- Native-heavy behavior still needs release-candidate device proof.
- Local AI requires installed model files and a compatible native build.
- Road routing requires downloaded routing packs for the selected region.
- Ark is not a substitute for emergency services, medical professionals, official local instructions, or verified field training.
